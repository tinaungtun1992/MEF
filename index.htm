Welcome to Minmahaw Education Foundation

Minmahaw Education Foundation (MEF) is a non-profit, education organization. It was opened in January 2007 on the Thai-Burma border, to educate Burmese youth with disadvantaged backgrounds. With current trends in the Burmese educational standard, and little access to education for the country’s ethnic minorities, border migrant community and youth from political backgrounds, further education is limited and in some cases simply unattainable.

The Road to the Future

MEF aims to provide higher level education, in order to prepare students to take a high school equivalency exam so they can gain entry to university. The program not only provides education, but also a safe location for the students to study and, as well as food, clothing and basic medical care.

The program started with 20 students from multi-ethnic backgrounds (Karen, Shan, Mon, Palaung, Chin, Arakanese and Burman) from both inside Burma and the Thai/ Burma border with the assistance of volunteer teachers from Burma and other countries. The students were divided into Elementary, Pre-Intermediate and Intermediate classes according to their level of English. The first year of the program (2007) was predominantly an English immersion and skills upgrade course. In 2008, the first group started preparation to gain an accredited high school certificate and in 2009, nine students will take this high school equivalency exam.
