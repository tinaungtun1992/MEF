We are counting on you....

Minmahaw Education Foundation depends upon you, our individual supporters, for the bulk of its income. The Post Ten program, Summer School program, Vocational training courses, the rural kindergarten project, are all funded by friends and supporters around the world, just like you.

We have the plans and people ready to expand our programs and make dreams come true. We are only waiting for the funds to appear. Your donation will go directly to help educate and improve the future of youth from Burma, who are truly thirsty for knowledge.

The opportunity to attain higher education is small or none at all for most students from Burma. The education of the new generation is vital for future of Burma – it is part of the fight for freedom and democracy.

Your donation will go a long way... for example:

  • $50 – will pay for the cost of one student attending school for one month, this includes food, board (accommodation, electricity, water), study materials and student supplies
  • $100 – will pay for student supplies and education materials (e.g. photocopies, stationery) for the school for one month
  • $200 – will pay for the boarding costs (accommodation, electricity, water) for one student for one year
  • $300 – will pay for food costs for one student for one year
  • $400 – will pay for educational materials (e.g. photocopies, stationery) for the school for one year
  • $600 – will pay for the total cost of a student attending school, which includes food, board (accommodation, electricity, water), study materials and student supplies
  • $1300 – will pay for the running costs of the school for one month
  • $2500 – will pay for one student to obtain travel documents, take the high school equivalency exam and receive a high school certificate, paving the way for their tertiary education
